What is ACC?

The Accident Compensation Corporation (ACC) provides injury cover for all New Zealand residents and visitors. If your condition is related to an accident or injury, you may be eligible for subsidized treatment.

Eligible Conditions

  • Sports injuries
  • Work-related injuries
  • Motor vehicle accidents
  • Slips, trips, and falls
  • Other accident-related injuries

How to Claim

  1. Visit your GP or contact us directly
  2. Your injury will be assessed for ACC eligibility
  3. If approved, you'll receive subsidized treatment
  4. Continue treatment as recommended
