Meet Dr. RUI ZHOU

Dr. RUI ZHOU is Auckland's premier sports acupuncture specialist, bringing over 15 years of expertise in Traditional Chinese Medicine with a specialized focus on sports injury recovery and athletic performance enhancement.

Professional Background

Dr. Zhou combines the ancient principles of Traditional Chinese Medicine with cutting-edge understanding of sports science and human physiology. His unique approach has helped thousands of athletes and active individuals achieve optimal performance and recovery.

Treatment Philosophy

Holistic Approach

Dr. Zhou believes in treating the whole person, not just the symptoms. By understanding the root cause of pain and dysfunction, he creates comprehensive treatment plans that address both immediate relief and long-term wellness.
